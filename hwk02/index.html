The Topgolf Experience

So what is Topgolf? In short, we’re a sports entertainment complex that features an inclusive, high-tech golf game that everyone can enjoy, paired with an outstanding food and beverage menu, all-weather hitting bays and music. Every Topgolf has an energetic hum that you can feel right when you walk through the door.

Here’s how Topgolf works:

Step1

Find Your Crew

Whether you’re a planner or a procrastinator, we’ve got options for everyone. Make a reservation in advance with our super simple online booking tool or walk right in and put your name down. Reservations aren’t required, though you may end up waiting longer during peak times. Groups of seven or more are encouraged to reserve bays in advance.

?

How much does Topgolf cost?

Each bay is priced by the hour, with the hourly rate varying by day and time, plus a $6 one-time member fee for new players. Up to 6 players can be in a bay and play. For safety reasons, only one person can be hitting at one time.

?

Does Topgolf offer any discounts?

Absolutely! View all the deals and promotions currently available at Topgolf, with no coupon cutting required.

?

Where can I buy Topgolf gift cards?

Our gift cards are good for anything in the building — for gear, game play, food, and even lessons. You can buy them online, at a Topgolf venue, or in thousands of retail locations nationwide.

Step2

Arrive & Check In

Upon arrival, check in with our front desk team who will find the perfect bay for you. Before or after you play, grab a drink in our full bar and restaurant.

?

Does Topgolf close due to weather?

Unless it’s an extreme weather event, nothing can stop a good time at Topgolf, not even rain or snow. Topgolf venues are all-weather facilities with covered hitting bays that are fitted with fans for the summer and heaters for the winter.

?

How much does it cost to rent golf clubs at Topgolf?

Zero dollars. Free. Nothing. All Topgolf clubs are complimentary to use during game play. We have clubs for men, ladies, left-handers, right-handers, kids, and toddlers for your convenience. Men’s and women’s clubs are already in each bay.

?

Can I bring my own clubs to Topgolf?

If you’re more comfortable playing with your own clubs, we have designated areas within our bays to store them. We will also store your clubs in our club room if we happen to be on a wait so that you can enjoy our venue without lugging them around.

Step3

Pick Your Game

Once you settle into your all-weather bay, a Bayhost will help you set up your game and create profiles for each player. The fun part is deciding which of the more than a dozen games to play first.

Step4

Crush It

Using our complimentary clubs or your own, take aim at the giant outfield targets and our high-tech balls will score themselves. While you’re playing, order food and drinks and catch the game on the HDTVs in your bay.
